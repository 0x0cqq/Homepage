High Performance Computing System in Graph Mining

2021.12-2022.10

Academic Rising Star Program

  • Supervised by Prof. Jidong Zhai
  • Worked on a graph mining system named GraphIndor

I worked on a graph mining system on heterogeneous hardware (CPU and GPU), trying to find algorithmic and architectural optimization for various graph mining applications. I mainly worked on the part of Frequent Subgraph Mining(FSM), which is to find the most frequently appeared patterns with given numbers of vertexes. In this part, I looked into the key feature of FSM that those frequent patterns form a tree by their vertex nums and apply dynamic parallel strategies for different search tree widths. In some extreme cases, it significantly improves efficiency by hundreds of times.
